# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Tool Rental . We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our tool rental and equipment sales application.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you use our rental and shop services:

- **Full name** (as on driver's license for rental agreements)
- **Phone number**
- **Email address**
- **Physical address** (for delivery and rental purposes)
- **Parish/Location** (for orders and deliveries)
- **Digital signature** (for rental agreements)
- **Driver's license photo** (for rental verification)
- **Photos of inventory items** (if you use inventory management features)

## 2. How We Collect Information

Information is collected when you:

- Complete a rental agreement
- Make a purchase from our shop
- Create or manage inventory items
- Contact us via phone, email, or WhatsApp
- Use the camera or photo library to upload images (with your permission)
- Submit contact forms or booking requests

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

- Process rental agreements and shop orders
- Verify your identity for equipment rentals
- Contact you regarding your rentals, purchases, or inquiries
- Send rental confirmations, receipts, and updates via email
- Arrange delivery or pickup of equipment
- Maintain records for legal and business purposes
- Improve our services and customer experience
- Manage inventory and business operations
- Comply with legal obligations

- Take photos of tools and equipment for inventory management -N/A

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction, including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy

Rental agreements and related information are typically retained for 7 years in accordance with business record-keeping requirements.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Request correction** of inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Request deletion** of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
- **Withdraw consent** for data collection at any time
- **Object to processing** of your information for certain purposes
- **Request data portability** (receive a copy of your data in a structured format)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
